Welcome to Asakusa, Tokyo

Asakusa is one of Tokyo's most famous and historic districts. It is known for its traditional atmosphere, beautiful temples, and lively shopping streets.

Main Attractions

  • Senso-ji Temple: Tokyo's oldest temple, featuring the iconic Kaminarimon (Thunder Gate) and a bustling approach.
  • Nakamise-dori: A street leading to the temple, filled with shops selling traditional snacks, crafts, and souvenirs.
  • Sumida Park: A beautiful spot along the Sumida River, perfect for cherry blossom viewing and river cruises.
